# Privacy Policy

**NutrientWise**

Last updated: February 2025

## Overview

NutrientWise is a nutritional reference app that runs entirely on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

## Data Collection

NutrientWise does **not** collect any data. Specifically:

- No personal information is collected
- No usage data or analytics are collected
- No tracking or advertising identifiers are used
- No data is sent to external servers

## Data Stored on Your Device

The app stores the following data locally on your device:

- Your language preference (English or French)
- Your selected nutritional profile
- Your list of favorite foods

If you have iCloud enabled, these preferences are synced across your Apple devices using Apple's built-in iCloud key-value storage. This data is managed entirely by Apple and is not accessible to us.

## Third-Party Services

NutrientWise does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## Children's Privacy

NutrientWise does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, the updated version will be posted here.
